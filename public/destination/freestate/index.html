Much more than Mielies

Detour off the N3 and discover high berg wilderness and cultural treasures. The Clarens area is famous for its fine restaurants, quirky art cafes, and more recently, its well preserved dinosaur fossils and San rock art. Our new 3 day slackpacking trail in the area will enable you to discover all these gems and more.
